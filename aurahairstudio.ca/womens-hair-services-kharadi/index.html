FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS

How often should I get a haircut?

We recommend getting a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends and keep your style fresh.

Whatâ€™s the difference between balayage and ombre?

Balayage involves hand-painting highlights for a natural, sun-kissed look, while ombre transitions from darker roots to lighter ends.

Do you offer consultations for hair colour?

Yes! We encourage consultations before any colour service at our hair salon for women to ensure we understand your goals and can recommend the best approach for your hair. Schedule your consultation online today!

Can I add a hair spa treatment to my haircut or color service?

Absolutely! Our hair spa treatments can be added to any service for extra nourishment and shine.

Are your hair products safe for color-treated hair?

Yes, we use high-quality, professional products that are safe and designed to enhance and protect color-treated hair.

How long does a typical appointment take?

The duration of your appointment depends on the service you’re receiving. For haircuts, it usually takes around 45 minutes to an hour. Colour treatments or hair spa services may take longer, typically 2-3 hours.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While we do accept walk-ins when available, we highly recommend booking your appointment in advance to ensure you secure your preferred time with your stylist.

How soon can I wash my hair after a color treatment?

We recommend waiting at least 48 hours before washing your hair after a colour treatment to allow the colour to be fully set. This helps preserve the vibrancy of the colour.

Do you offer hair services for special events?

Yes, at our hair salon for women, we offer a variety of styling options for special occasions, including weddings, parties, and formal events. You can book a consultation to discuss your desired style.

Are your services suitable for all hair types?

Yes, our stylists are trained to work with all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and textured hair. We tailor each service to meet the specific needs of your hair.

Eyebrow tinting is a treatment that uses a semi-permanent dye to enhance the colour and shape of your eyebrows, giving them a fuller definition.

How long does eyebrow tinting last?

Typically, eyebrow tinting lasts around 3-4 weeks, depending on your skin type and care routine.
