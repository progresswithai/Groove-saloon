What’s the Average Price for a Women’s Haircut in Innisfil?

In Innisfil, women’s haircuts generally range from $40 to $90, depending on the level of service. Some budget salons may offer cuts closer to the $30–$40 mark, but this usually includes a basic cut with minimal styling. Mid-range salons tend to charge between $60 and $75, while premium salons or senior stylists may charge upwards of $80 to $90 for a full haircut and styling experience.

Aura Hair Studio sits in the mid-to-premium range. Why? Because we offer more than just a cut—we focus on hair health, style, and giving you a look that suits your features, lifestyle, and goals. Our prices reflect the quality of service, the skill of our stylists, and the products we use.

We are a trusted local destination for high-quality women’s hair services in Innisfil, from expert cuts to nourishing treatments and vibrant colour.

How Experience Impacts the Price

Like in most industries, experience matters. Stylists with more training, advanced certifications, and years behind the chair often charge more—and for good reason. They bring deeper knowledge, stronger technical skills, and the confidence to handle everything from classic cuts to complex restyles.

At Aura Hair Studio, we have a team of experienced stylists who stay up to date with current trends and cutting methods. Whether you want something timeless or modern, you’re in good hands. We match you with a stylist whose skills align with your vision and hair type.

Short Hair vs Long Hair: Does It Change the Price?

Yes, sometimes. Cutting long hair usually takes more time, especially if there’s layering, texturizing, or reshaping involved. Short styles, while precise, can be quicker to execute. At Aura Hair Studio, we keep pricing fair based on the time and detail required—not just the length.

So, if you have long hair and want to reshape it with layers and style, the price may be slightly higher than a maintenance trim. But we always clarify pricing during your consultation, so there are no surprises.

Add-Ons That May Affect the Final Price

Haircuts are often part of a bigger picture. Some clients book additional services that add to the overall cost. Here are some popular add-ons at Aura Hair Studio:

  • Deep conditioning treatments
     These restore moisture, repair damage, and add shine. Especially useful if your hair is dry, coloured, or heat-damaged.
  • Gloss or toner
     Helps refresh colour between appointments or boost shine.
  • Blowout upgrade
     Want extra volume or a sleek finish? An upgraded blowout can be added to your service.
  • Split-end repair treatment
     A popular option for those growing their hair but wanting clean, healthy ends.

Adding one or two of these treatments can take your total from $70 to $120 or more—but you’re also getting more than just a trim. You’re investing in the health and look of your hair.

Why Cheap Isn’t Always Better

We get it—saving money is important. But with haircuts, you often get what you pay for. A poorly done cut can be hard to fix, take months to grow out, and leave you frustrated. Choosing a professional salon like Aura Hair Studio gives you peace of mind. We take the time, use professional tools and products, and have the training to deliver great results consistently.

Plus, a great haircut grows out better. That means you can go longer between cuts, which saves money in the long run.

How Often Should You Get a Haircut?

For most women, we recommend a haircut every 8 to 12 weeks, depending on your style and hair health. Short styles may need trimming more often to keep their shape. If you’re growing your hair, you can space your cuts out a bit more, but regular trims help prevent split ends and breakage.

During your appointment, your stylist will let you know what schedule is best for your hair and goals.
