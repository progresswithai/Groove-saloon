]]> At Aura Hair Studio in Innisfil, staying ahead of style trends isn’t a bonus but an expectation. Whether you’re after a subtle update or a major transformation, knowing which haircuts are trending helps you make a confident choice. In 2025, we’re seeing a blend of timeless classics, modern twists, and low-maintenance looks designed to match busy lifestyles and personal expression.

Here are the top 10 most requested haircuts we’re seeing for 2025, perfect for our clients in Innisfil and the surrounding areas.

1. Blunt Bob

The blunt bob continues to dominate. Sleek, sharp, and sophisticated, it’s perfect for clients who want a polished, professional look without a lot of styling fuss.

Why it’s popular:

  • Easy to maintain with regular trims
  • Works on straight, wavy, and slightly curly hair
  • Pairs beautifully with bold hair colours like platinum blonde, jet black, or chocolate brown

Pro tip: Consider adding a glass hair finish treatment to your blunt bob for a mirror-like shine.

2. Textured Lob (Long Bob)

If you want a bit more length without sacrificing style, the textured lob is the answer. This shoulder-skimming cut offers movement and softness, making it a top pick.

Why it’s popular:

  • Versatile for updos, half-up styles, or loose waves
  • Flatters round, oval, and heart-shaped faces
  • Great canvas for balayage, babylights, or sombre techniques

Styling tip: Use a light wave spray to enhance the natural texture without making the hair heavy.

3. Soft Layered Shag

The 70s-inspired shag is back but with a modern, softer update. Think lots of movement, feathered layers, and a slightly tousled finish.

Why it’s popular:

  • Enhances natural texture (perfect for wavy and curly hair)
  • Requires minimal daily styling effort
  • Adds effortless volume and shape

Styling tip: Diffuse dry with a curl cream or texturizing spray for an easy, lived-in look.

4. Curtain Bangs with Long Layers

Curtain bangs are the ultimate face-framer. Paired with long layers, this style is flattering and forgiving, blending easily with the rest of the haircut as it grows.

Why it’s popular:

  • Softens facial features and forehead lines
  • Easy to style or grow out gracefully
  • Pairs well with all hair textures

Maintenance tip: Trim curtain bangs every 4–6 weeks to keep them in perfect shape without needing a full haircut.

5. Pixie Cut

The pixie is making a strong return in 2025, offering a bold, fresh start for clients looking to simplify their routine.

Why it’s popular:

  • Quick styling time – ideal for busy mornings
  • Highlights facial structure like cheekbones and jawlines
  • Can be styled sleek, messy, or spiky depending on the occasion

Styling tip: Use a lightweight pomade or texturizing wax for definition without weighing the hair down.

6. French Bob

Shorter than the classic bob and usually worn with a slight wave or natural texture, the French bob exudes effortless chic, perfect for Innisfil’s stylish crowd.

Why it’s popular:

  • Easy to air-dry for a natural look
  • Flattering for petite frames and heart-shaped faces
  • Works beautifully with natural hair colours or subtle highlights

Bonus: Adding soft bangs to your French bob can enhance its romantic feel.

7. Butterfly Cut

The butterfly cut features long, cascading layers starting around the chin and floating downward, giving the illusion of two different lengths.

Why it’s popular:

  • Creates major movement and volume without sacrificing length
  • Perfect for heavy or thick hair that needs lightness
  • Highly customizable to face shape and personal style

Pro tip: Add subtle face-framing highlights to accentuate the layers and create even more dimension.

8. Choppy Bob with Micro Bangs

For the bold and fashion-forward, the choppy bob paired with micro bangs is a statement look that’s turning heads.

Why it’s popular:

  • Unique and edgy yet still wearable
  • Emphasizes facial features, especially the eyes and cheekbones
  • Versatile – can be styled sleek or tousled

Colour tip: Pair with pastel tones or vivid shades like copper, lavender, or icy blonde for an even more striking look.

9. Long, Blunt Layers

Clients who want to keep their length but add subtle shape are loving long, blunt layers. The result is a strong, healthy-looking silhouette with minimal fuss.

Why it’s popular:

  • Maintains fullness at the ends
  • Adds visual interest without compromising length
  • Easy to style straight, curled, or naturally wavy

Maintenance tip: Use regular deep conditioning treatments to keep the ends healthy and prevent split ends from dulling the shape.

10. Modern Mullet

Yes, the mullet is back, but it’s more stylish and wearable than ever. The modern mullet features softer transitions between shorter fronts and longer backs, creating a laid-back yet high-fashion feel.

Why it’s popular:

  • Bold and fashion-forward
  • Works with various textures, from pin-straight to curly
  • Tailored to be as subtle or edgy as you like

Styling tip: Use mousse or sea salt spray to enhance natural movement and keep the style airy rather than stiff.
