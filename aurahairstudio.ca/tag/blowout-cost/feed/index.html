What Is a Professional Blowout?

A blowout is a salon service where a stylist washes, conditions, and styles your hair using a blow-dryer and round brushes. Unlike a quick at-home dry, a professional blowout delivers volume, smoothness, and shine that lasts for days.

Stylists often finish the look with straighteners, curling irons, or finishing products to lock in the style.

Factors That Affect Blowout Prices

The cost of your blowout can vary for several reasons:

1. Hair Length and Thickness

Longer or thicker hair requires more time and effort to dry and style. This can add a small surcharge compared to shorter hair.

2. Stylist Expertise

Senior stylists with more experience often charge slightly higher rates. Their knowledge of techniques and ability to recommend styling suited to your hair type makes the service worth the price.

3. Products Used

Salons like Aura Hair Studio use professional-grade shampoos, conditioners, and finishing sprays. These improve results and protect your hair from heat and damage.

4. Add-On Treatments

Pairing a blowout with treatments or deep moisture masks can increase the cost but also improve hair health and longevity of style.

5. Location

Prices in Innisfil are generally lower compared to big city salons in Toronto. This makes a blowout in Innisfil a more affordable luxury.

How Long Does a Blowout Last?

The longevity of your blowout depends on your hair type and how you care for it afterward. On average, results last three to five days. Tips to extend your blowout include:

  • Sleeping on a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz.
  • Using dry shampoo to refresh roots.
  • Avoiding humidity and excessive sweating.
  • Wrapping or pinning hair at night to preserve volume.

How to Maintain Your Blowout at Home

Stylists often recommend a few tricks to extend the life of your blowout:

  1. Dry Shampoo – Keeps roots fresh and prevents oil buildup.
  2. Loose Hairstyles – Use soft hair ties or clips to avoid creases.
  3. Humidity Protection – Anti-frizz sprays help combat weather changes.
  4. Gentle Brushing – Use a boar-bristle brush to smooth without damage.

These small habits keep your hair looking like you just left the salon.

Are blowouts suitable for all hair types?

Absolutely. From fine to thick, straight to curly, blowouts can be adapted for any hair type.

How long does a blowout appointment take?

Yes. Blowouts are not limited to women. Men with medium to long hair benefit from the same polished finish.
